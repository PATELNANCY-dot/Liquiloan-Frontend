About Liquiloans

Liquiloans is a technology-driven Indian fintech platform transforming the way individuals lend and borrow through Peer-to-Peer lending. Our platform connects creditworthy borrowers with lenders seeking better, inflation-adjusted returns — all through a secure, fully digital, and transparent ecosystem.

By leveraging advanced credit assessment models, data analytics, and risk-based borrower profiling, Liquiloans ensures investments are intelligently diversified to reduce risk and enhance stability.

  • Transparent Lending Opportunities
  • Automated Risk Assessment & Credit Grading
  • Paperless Onboarding & KYC
  • Diversified Lending Portfolios
  • Attractive Returns vs Traditional Lending

Our mission is to democratize credit access while empowering investors with smarter, data-backed investment solutions.

Liquiloans is transforming peer-to-peer lending in India — smarter, safer, and more rewarding.
