Manage Account

CHANGE OF EMAIL ID

Change email id on Liquiloan – Online process

  • Enter your new email id
  • Enter OTP received on new email id
  • On entering OTP, new email id will be updated in Liquiloan
Note: Make sure your new email id is active and accessible to receive OTP.
CHANGE OF MOBILE NUMBER

Change mobile number on Liquiloan – Online process

  • Enter your new mobile number
  • Enter OTP received on new mobile number
  • On entering OTP, your mobile number will be updated in Liquiloan
Note: Make sure your new mobile number is active to receive OTP.
CHANGE OF BANK DETAILS

Update your bank details on Liquiloan – Online process

  • Enter your new bank account details
  • Provide correct IFSC and branch information
  • Verify your bank details before submission
  • Submit the updated bank details
Note: Ensure the bank account belongs to you to avoid transaction issues.
CHANGE OF NOMINEE

Update nominee details on Liquiloan – Online process

  • Add or update nominee information
  • Ensure total nominee percentage equals 100%
  • Provide guardian details if nominee is a minor (below 18)
  • Submit the updated nominee details
Note: You can add up to 3 nominees. For minors, guardian details are mandatory.
